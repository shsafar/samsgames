We're excited to introduce **DiskBreak**, a challenging spatial logic puzzle game!

### About DiskBreak
Rebuild broken disks by dragging scattered fragments back to their original positions. Test your spatial reasoning and puzzle-solving skills with three difficulty levels.

### Game Features
- **Daily Puzzles**: New puzzle every day with consistent seed-based generation
- **Three Difficulty Levels**:
  - **Level 1 (Easy)**: 1 disk, 5 chords, no timer - perfect for learning
  - **Level 2 (Medium)**: 1 disk, 7 chords, 90-second timer
  - **Level 3 (Hard)**: 2 disks, 7 chords each, 90-second timer, same gradient color
- **Rotating Schedule**: Difficulty varies by day of the week
  - Monday, Wednesday, Friday: Level 1 (Easy)
  - Tuesday, Thursday: Level 2 (Medium)
  - Saturday, Sunday: Level 3 (Hard)

### Gameplay Mechanics
- **Drag & Drop**: Intuitive touch controls for mobile devices
- **Smart Snap**: Pieces automatically snap when close to correct position
- **Hint System**: Temporarily shows crack lines to help identify piece positions
- **Scoring**:
  - +10 points per piece (Level 1)
  - +15 points per piece (Levels 2 & 3)
  - Time bonus: +1 point per 5 seconds remaining
  - Hint penalty: -5 points (Level 1), -10 points (Levels 2 & 3)

### Mobile Optimizations
