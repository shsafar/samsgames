## üÜï What's New

### WordStacks - Shape-Based Word Puzzle
A fresh take on word puzzles where you solve words arranged in geometric patterns!

**Game Features:**
- **21 Words to Solve**: Progress through increasingly challenging vocabulary
- **4 Shape Types**: Triangle (3 letters), Square (4 letters), Pentagon (5 letters), and Hexagon (6 letters)
- **Shared Letters**: Words connect through shared letters, creating an interconnected tower
- **Smart Difficulty**: Hidden letters are randomized based on word length
- **Helpful Clues**: Each word comes with a contextual hint
- **120-Second Timer**: Race against the clock to complete all words
- **Sound Effects**: Pleasant audio feedback for solving words and completing the game
- **Sound Toggle**: Control audio with the üîä/üîá button

**How to Play:**
1. Fill in the missing letters in each geometric shape to form valid words
2. Use the clues on the right side of each shape to guide you
3. Solve the current word to unlock the next shape
4. Shared letters (highlighted in gold) connect words together
5. Complete all 21 words before time runs out!

**Game Controls:**
- **Reveal**: Show all letters in the current word
- **Next Word**: Manually advance to the next word (after solving current)
- **Sound**: Toggle sound effects on/off
- **Restart/New Game**: Hidden in daily mode (play once per day!)
